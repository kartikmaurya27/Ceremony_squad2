Our Story

Fifteen Years of
Crafting Legends

"We don't just plan events — we architect memories."

Lumière Events was born in 2009 in a small studio in Sector 63, Noida, with nothing more than a vision: to bring the kind of breathtaking event experiences seen at international venues to India's most discerning families and organisations.

What began as a two-person wedding coordination service grew — through relentless craft, word-of-mouth and an obsession with quality — into India's most trusted full-service luxury event company. Today, our 50-member team orchestrates over 200 events a year across the country and beyond.

"Every event is a chapter in someone's life story. We take that responsibility with the deepest reverence — and the boldest imagination." — Arjun Lumière, Founder & Creative Director

From intimate Kashmiri destination weddings to 25,000-capacity concerts at the Buddh International Circuit, we operate at every scale — always with the same obsessive attention to detail that made us famous.

Founder & Creative Director

Arjun Lumière — born Arjun Sharma in Noida — is the visionary founder and creative force behind India's most prestigious event studio. Educated at the National Institute of Design, Ahmedabad, and trained under legendary event producers in London and Dubai, Arjun returned to India in 2009 with a mission: to create a world-class event company rooted in Indian culture but boundless in global ambition.

With an architectural background that informs his obsessive spatial thinking, Arjun personally art-directs every flagship event the studio takes on. His philosophy — that an event is not a service but a living work of art — has redefined what Indian families and organisations expect from their celebrations.

Difference

We aren't the cheapest option — and we don't try to be. We are the most trusted option for clients who understand that their event is a once-in-a-lifetime investment, and who refuse to compromise on the result. Here is exactly why over 2,400 clients have chosen Lumière, and why 70% of our business comes from referrals and repeat bookings.
01

15 Years of Mastery

There is no substitute for experience. 15 years means we've faced every disaster scenario imaginable — and solved it invisibly before you noticed. Our instincts are sharper, our foresight deeper, our network wider than any newer competitor.

02

In-House Creative Team

Unlike most event companies that outsource design to freelancers, our 12-person in-house creative team — designers, 3D visualisers and décor artists — means your concept is developed, refined and owned entirely by us. Faster, better, more consistent.

03

Named Director, Not a Call Centre

Every client gets a named senior event director as their single point of contact — available by phone, WhatsApp and email 24/7. No tickets, no queues, no call centres. Just a real human who knows your event inside out and is accountable to you directly.

04

120+ Vetted Vendor Partners

Our vendor network took a decade to build. Every partner has been personally vetted, tested on real events and is contractually bound to our quality standards. When you book Lumière, you get the entire ecosystem — not just the planning.

05

Zero Surprises Policy

We are India's only event company with a written Zero Surprises Guarantee. Every cost is itemised in your contract upfront. No day-of surprise charges, no "additional fees". What you sign is exactly what you pay — including a 5% contingency buffer we build in to protect you.

06

Sustainable Luxury Events

We are India's first luxury event company to achieve carbon-neutral certification for events over 500 guests. We use compostable décor elements, zero single-use plastics and calculate and offset all event carbon footprints automatically.
