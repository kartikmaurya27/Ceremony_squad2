Questions

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 6–12 months in advance for weddings and large-scale concerts. For smaller events like birthdays and corporate functions, 2–3 months notice is usually sufficient.
Do you handle destination weddings?
Absolutely. We have planned weddings across Udaipur, Goa, Jaipur, Kerala and internationally in Dubai and Thailand. Our team handles all local vendor coordination and logistics.
What is included in your packages?
All packages include a dedicated event director, on-site coordination, vendor management and post-event support. Specific inclusions vary by tier — please see the pricing section above.
Can I customise a package?
Yes — every engagement is tailored to your exact needs. We can build a fully bespoke package from scratch or modify any of our existing tiers to suit your budget and vision.
Do you work outside Delhi NCR?
Yes. We have delivered events across Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Jaipur, Udaipur, Goa and multiple international destinations. Travel and accommodation for our team is factored into your quote.
What is your payment structure?
We typically take a 30% booking deposit, 40% at 30 days before the event, and the final 30% on the day of the event. Flexible payment plans are available for large events.
Do you provide catering?
Yes, catering is available as an add-on through our curated partner caterers. We offer everything from traditional Indian thalis to international fine dining and live cooking stations.
How do I get started?
Simply fill out our contact form or call us. A senior event director will reach out within 4 hours to schedule a discovery call and begin crafting your vision.
